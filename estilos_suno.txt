# Estilos Musicais Suno (Máx: ~120 caracteres)
# O script de geração em massa irá listar esses estilos base e adicionar os tipos vocais.

[Worship Balada]
Worship melody, piano intro, slow build-up, acoustic guitar, powerful stadium drums, epic chorus

[Blues Gospel]
Slow blues gospel, electric guitar solo, soulful bass, hammond organ, raw emotion, steady rhythmic beat

[Sertanejo Gospel]
Sertanejo universitario, acoustic guitar, accordion, upbeat rhythm, joyful acoustic drums

[Forró Gospel]
Upbeat forro, accordion, zabumba, triangle, energetic rhythm, rhythmic bass, dancing acoustic beat

[Rock Cristão]
Christian rock, distorted electric guitars, heavy driving drums, energetic build up, epic solo

[Reggae Gospel]
Roots reggae, smooth off-beat guitar, deep dub bass, light percussion, rhythmic organ, peaceful

[Gospel Épico]
Epic orchestral gospel, massive cinematic choir, grand piano intro, huge brass section, triumphant build-up, emotional

[Hino Tradicional]
Traditional hymn, grand church organ, reverent choir harmony, classical sopranos, solemn pace, majestic atmosphere

[Pop Contemporâneo]
Modern contemporary pop, synth pop beats, bright acoustic guitar, dynamic electronic drums

[Rap Gospel]
Hip hop beat, rhythmic spoken word, deep 808 bass, boom bap drums, soulful background singing
